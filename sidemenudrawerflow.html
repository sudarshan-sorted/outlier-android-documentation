Side Menu Drawer Flow

The Side Menu Drawer allows users to access key content sections, team information, media content, and legal or settings-related screens. The drawer content dynamically changes based on whether the user is logged in or logged out.

1. Drawer Overview

The Side Menu Drawer provides global navigation across the application.
It slides in from the side when opened and displays navigation options based on the user’s authentication state.

2. Logged Out State

When the user is not logged in, the following options are displayed in the drawer:

  • News
    Opens the News screen where all news articles can be browsed.

  • Where to Watch
    Opens the Where to Watch screen.

  • Teams
    Expands a list of all teams.

    • Selecting a team opens the Team Details screen.

  • URC TV
    Opens the URC TV website inside an in-app web view.

3. Bottom Navigation Section (Logged Out)

The bottom section of the drawer contains:

  • Settings
    Opens the Settings screen.

  • About
    Opens the About screen.

  • Terms and Conditions
    Opens the Terms and Conditions screen.

  • Privacy Policy
    Opens the Privacy Policy screen.

  • Cookie Policy
    Opens the Cookie Policy screen.

  • Login
    Opens the Login screen.

4. Logged In State

When the user is logged in, the drawer displays additional personalized navigation:

  • News
    Opens the News screen where all news articles can be browsed.

  • My Team
    Opens the My Team Details screen.

  • Where to Watch
    Opens the Where to Watch screen.

  • Teams
    Expands a list of all teams.

    • Selecting a team opens the Team Details screen.

  • URC TV
    Opens the URC TV website inside an in-app web view.

5. Bottom Navigation Section (Logged In)

When logged in, the bottom section contains:

  • Settings
    Opens the Settings screen.

  • About
    Opens the About screen.

  • Terms and Conditions
    Opens the Terms and Conditions screen.

  • Privacy Policy
    Opens the Privacy Policy screen.

  • Cookie Policy
    Opens the Cookie Policy screen.

  • Logout
    Logs the user out and returns the drawer to the logged-out state.

6. User Interaction Summary

  • User opens the Side Menu Drawer.

  • Drawer displays navigation options based on authentication state.

  • User selects navigation items to open respective screens.

  • Teams and My Team provide navigation to team-specific detail screens.

  • Login and Logout change the drawer state dynamically.
