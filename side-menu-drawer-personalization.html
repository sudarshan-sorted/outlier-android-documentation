Side Menu Drawer

This section covers the personalized navigation options for logged-in users. It focuses only on items that differ from the standard flow but keeps enough context for clarity.

1. Personalized Drawer Options

In addition to the standard drawer items, logged-in users see:

  • My Team
    Provides quick access to the user's favorite team.
    Opens the My Team Details screen, allowing the user to view team news, fixtures, and stats.

Note: All other drawer options (News, Teams, URC TV, Where to Watch) remain the same as the standard flow.

2. Bottom Navigation Section (Personalized)

The bottom navigation includes a Logout button in place of the Login button:

  • Logout
    Logs the user out and switches the drawer back to the standard navigation state.

Note: Other bottom navigation items (Settings, About, Terms and Conditions, Privacy Policy, Cookie Policy) are unchanged from standard flow.
