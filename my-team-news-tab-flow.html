News Tab Flow

The News tab in My Team displays news articles specifically related to the user’s favourite team. It is a scoped version of the global News screen with team-based filtering applied.

1. Default State

  • News tab is selected from the My Team Tab Selector.

  • Team-specific news articles are displayed in a vertical list.

2. Team News List

  • A vertical list of team-related news articles is displayed.

  • Each article card typically contains:

    • Article title

    • Cover image

    • Publication date

3. Article Interaction

  • Clicking on any article opens the Article Detail Screen, which displays:

    • Article title

    • Publication date

    • Cover image

    • Full article content

4. View All Action

  • A View All button is shown in the My Team News tab.

  • Clicking View All opens the global News Screen, where all URC news articles can be browsed (not limited to the favourite team).

5. User Flow Summary

  1. User opens My Team → News tab.

  2. Team-specific news articles are displayed in a list.

  3. User taps an article to open the Article Detail Screen.

  4. User taps View All to navigate to the global News screen.
