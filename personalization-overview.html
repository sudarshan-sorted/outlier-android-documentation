Overview

This section introduces personalization in the URC Android app and explains how the app tailors content and features based on user preferences, primarily focusing on the favorite team selected during account creation.

1. How Personalization Works

  • The app fetches personalized data via GraphQL requests.

  • User preferences, such as the favorite team, are used to tailor content throughout the app.

2. Areas of Personalization

  • Multiple sections and flows within the app display content adapted to the user’s favorite team.

  • Whenever the favorite team is present, the interface adjusts to highlight relevant information, providing a more engaging experience.

3. Purpose of this Section

This overview serves as a foundation for understanding where and how personalized data is presented across the app. Subsequent sections will detail the flows and interactions in which personalized content is surfaced, without referencing any code or technical implementation.
