Home Screen Flow

The Home screen serves as the landing page once the user has successfully logged in (or accessed the app as a guest). It provides personalized and general content, quick access to account information, and navigation to match and story details.

1. Top Bar

  • Sticky top bar that remains visible as the user scrolls.

  • Left: Side drawer opener/closer button for additional navigation options.

  • Center: URC logo.

  • Right: Account icon.

Account Icon Behavior:

  • If the user is logged in: Opens myaccount.unitedrugby.com.

  • If not logged in: Redirects to the authentication portal for login or account creation.

2. Stories Component

  • Instagram-like circular stories displayed at the top.

  • Logged-in users: Shows highlights from the user’s favorite teams.

  • Guest users: Shows general match highlights.

  • Interaction: Clicking a story opens a viewer similar to Instagram stories, allowing sequential viewing.

With Login / Personalization

3. Matches Component

  • Displays live, past and upcoming matches in horizontal card lists.

  • Logged-in users with favorite teams: Fixtures of their selected favorite teams are shown prominently.

  • All users (including guests or those without favorite teams): A horizontal list of general matches is always displayed.

  • Interaction: Clicking on a match card opens the Match Details screen, showing complete match information.

Without Login / Personalization

With Login / Personalization

4. Ads Banner 1

  • Displayed on the Home screen immediately after the Matches cards.

  • Location-enabled users: Shows regional-specific advertisements tailored to the user's location.

  • Users without location permission: Displays general ads applicable to all regions.

  • Banners are interactive and clickable, leading users to external or in-app content as defined by the advertiser.

5. Headlines Component

  • Positioned on the Home screen after Ads Banner 1.

  • Displays key headlines for all users.

  • Interaction:

    • Clicking on a headline opens the full article.

    • A "View All" button navigates to the News Activity, showing the complete list of news articles.

6. Superstars Section

  • Displayed on the Home screen as a horizontal card list.

  • Features player highlights and top-performing athletes.

  • Interaction: Clicking on a player card opens the Stories viewer, allowing the user to see highlights and stories related to that player.

7. Ads Banner 2

  • Positioned immediately after the Superstars section.

  • Functions similarly to Ads Banner 1, displaying regional or general advertisements depending on location permissions.

  • Banners are interactive and clickable, leading users to external or in-app content as defined by the advertiser.

8. Favorites & Standings Section

This section adapts based on the user’s login and personalization status.

Logged-In Users

  • Favorite Team Banner: Highlights the user's selected favorite team prominently.

  • Headlines Component: Displays the latest news and updates related to the user's favorite team.

  • Standings Component: Shows the top 3 teams in the league, always highlighting the user's favorite team.

  • Results Component: Displays the results of past matches for the user's favorite team where outcomes have been declared.

Guest / Not Logged-In Users

  • Standings Component: Shows the top 3 teams in the league without highlighting any team.

  • Favorite team banners, headlines, and results are not personalized or displayed.

9. News Section

  • Located on the Home screen after the Favorites & Standings section.

  • Displays generic news content applicable to all users.

  • Interaction:

    • Clicking on a news card opens the detailed news view.

    • A "View All" button allows users to navigate to the News Activity, which shows a complete list of all news articles.
