Home Screen Personalization

The Home screen adapts its content based on the user’s login state and selected favorite teams.

Stories Personalization

  • Stories highlight content related to the user’s selected favorite teams.

  • Personalized stories are prioritized over generic match highlights.

Matches Personalization

  • Fixtures related to the user’s selected favorite teams are shown prominently in the matches list.

  • Personalized fixtures are prioritized in the horizontal card list.

Favorites & Standings Personalization

  • Favorite Team Banner: Highlights the user’s selected favorite team.

  • Team Headlines: Displays news and updates related to the favorite team.

  • Standings Highlight: The favorite team is highlighted in the standings list.

  • Results: Past match results for the favorite team are shown when available.
