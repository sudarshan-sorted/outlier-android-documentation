News Screen Flow

The News Screen acts as the central hub for all URC-related news and editorial content within the app. It aggregates key headlines and the latest articles, allowing users to stay updated with league announcements, match reports, and feature stories.

Entry Points

The News Screen can be accessed from multiple locations in the app:

  • Side Drawer → News

  • Home Screen → Headlines → View All

  • Home Screen → Latest News → View All

All entry points navigate to the same News listing screen.

Top Bar

  • Left: Back icon

    • Navigates to the previous screen.

  • Center Title: News

  • Right: Account icon

Account Icon Behavior:

  • If the user is logged in: Opens myaccount.unitedrugby.com.

  • If not logged in: Redirects to the authentication portal for login or account creation.

Headlines Pager Component

  • Positioned below the top bar.

  • Displays featured / key headlines in a horizontal pager.

  • User can swipe left or right to view multiple headline cards.

  • Each headline card is tappable and opens the corresponding news article.

News List Section

  • Displays a vertical list of news articles from URC.

  • Each news item includes:

    • Article thumbnail / cover image

    • Article title

    • Publish date

Pagination / Load More Behavior

  • By default, only a limited number of articles are loaded initially.

  • At the bottom of the list, a Show More button is displayed.

  • Tapping Show More loads additional articles into the list.

Article Detail Flow

Opening an Article

  • Tapping any article (from Headlines pager or News list) opens the Article Detail Screen.

Article Detail Layout

  • Article Title

  • Publish Date

  • Cover Image

  • Article Content Body:

    • Full formatted article text

    • Images and embedded media (if present)

User Interaction Flow Summary

  1. User navigates to News from Drawer or Home sections.

  2. News screen opens with Top Bar and Headlines pager.

  3. User scrolls through the news list.

  4. User taps Show More to load more articles.

  5. User taps an article to open full article details.

  6. User presses Back to return to the News list.
