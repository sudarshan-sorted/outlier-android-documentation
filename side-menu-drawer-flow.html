Side Menu Drawer Flow

The Side Menu Drawer allows users to access key content sections, team information, media content, and settings-related screens.

1. Drawer Overview

The Side Menu Drawer provides global navigation across the application.
It slides in from the side and shows navigation options for the user.

2. Drawer Options

The drawer contains the following options:

  • News
    Opens the News screen where all news articles can be browsed.

  • Where to Watch
    Opens the Where to Watch screen.

  • Teams
    Expands a list of all teams.

    • Selecting a team opens the Team Details screen.

  • URC TV
    Opens the URC TV website inside an in-app web view.

3. Bottom Navigation Section

The bottom section of the drawer contains:

  • Settings
    Opens the Settings screen.

  • About
    Opens the About screen.

  • Terms and Conditions
    Opens the Terms and Conditions screen.

  • Privacy Policy
    Opens the Privacy Policy screen.

  • Cookie Policy
    Opens the Cookie Policy screen.

  • Login
    Opens the Login screen.

4. User Interaction Summary

  • User opens the Side Menu Drawer.

  • Drawer displays navigation options.

  • User selects navigation items to open respective screens.

  • Teams provide navigation to team-specific detail screens.

  • Login button opens the authentication flow.
