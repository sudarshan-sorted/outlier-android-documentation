Ads Banner Flow

Ads banners are displayed across multiple screens in the application, including the Home screen and other content-driven surfaces. Ads content is resolved dynamically based on the user’s detected region when available.

Ads Resolution Flow

1. Region Detected

  • The app detects the user’s country or region using location services.

  • The ads system returns region-specific ad campaigns.

  • Ads may include localized promotions, campaigns, or region-relevant content.

  • These ads are rendered in predefined ad slots across the app UI.

2. Region Not Detected

  • If the user’s region cannot be detected, the app requests generic ad campaigns.

  • Generic ads are shown without regional targeting.

  • This ensures ad slots are always populated even when location data is unavailable.

Ads Placement Flow

Ads banners can appear in multiple sections of the app, including:

  • Home screen between major content sections

  • Other content-driven surfaces where ad slots are defined

Placement is controlled by the UI layout, while ad content is resolved dynamically by the ads platform.

User Flow

  • User opens Home or other content-driven screens.

  • The app resolves ads based on detected region when available.

  • Ads banners are rendered in predefined ad slots.

  • User can tap an ad banner to navigate to external or in-app content.

User Interaction Flow Summary

  • User opens content-driven screens containing ad slots.

  • System resolves ads based on detected region when available.

  • System falls back to generic ads when region is unavailable.

  • User views and interacts with ad banners displayed in the UI.
