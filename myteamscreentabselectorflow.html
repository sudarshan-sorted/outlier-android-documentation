Tab Selector Flow

The Tab Selector in the My Team section is used to switch between different team-specific sub-screens. It is positioned below the Team Overview Card and remains persistent across My Team.

1. Tab Selector Location

  • Positioned directly below the Team Overview Card.

  • Visible on all My Team sub-screens.

2. Tabs Available

The Tab Selector contains three tabs:

  1. News

  2. Fixtures

  3. Videos

3. Tab Behavior

  • Only one tab can be active at a time.

  • Switching tabs updates the content area below the selector without leaving the My Team section.

  • Tab selection state is visually highlighted.

4. Sub-Screen Mapping

Each tab opens a dedicated My Team sub-screen:

4.1 News Tab

  • Displays news articles related to the user’s favourite team.

4.2 Fixtures Tab

  • Displays team fixtures and results, depending on the selected season:

    • Current season → upcoming fixtures

    • Previous seasons → completed match results

4.3 Videos Tab

  • Displays team-related video stories and highlights.

5. User Flow Summary

  1. User opens My Team.

  2. Team Overview Card is displayed.

  3. User taps News / Fixtures / Videos tabs.

  4. Corresponding sub-screen content is rendered below the Tab Selector.
