Video Screen Flow

The Videos screen displays match highlights and video stories from all matches.

1. Top Bar

  • Sticky top bar is present as in Home.

  • Center title changes to "Videos".

  • Left and right icons remain consistent with Home behavior.

2. Highlights Component

  • Displays match highlights in the same Instagram-like story circles as the Home screen.

  • Shows highlights of all matches, without personalization.

  • Interaction: Clicking on a story opens the Stories viewer, allowing sequential viewing of highlights.

3. Ads Banner 1

  • Positioned on the Videos screen immediately after the Highlights component.

  • Location-enabled users: Shows regional-specific advertisements tailored to the user's location.

  • Users without location permission: Displays general ads applicable to all regions.

  • Banners are interactive and clickable, leading users to external or in-app content as defined by the advertiser.

4. Superstars Section

  • Displayed on the Videos screen as a horizontal card list, similar to the Home screen.

  • Features player highlights and top-performing athletes.

  • Interaction: Clicking on a player card opens the Stories viewer, allowing the user to see highlights and stories related to that player.

5. Ads Banner 2

  • Positioned immediately after the Superstars section on the Videos screen.

  • Functions similarly to Ads Banner 1, displaying regional or general advertisements depending on location permissions.

  • Banners are interactive and clickable, leading users to external or in-app content as defined by the advertiser.

6. Best of URC Section

  • Displayed on the Videos screen after Ads Banner 2.

  • Features a grid of video cards showcasing the best moments, highlights, and fun clips from URC matches.

  • Each card includes:

    • Title of the clip (e.g., "Powerful Props", "Precise 50:22s", "Best Try Assists", "Fun Moments")

    • Visual thumbnail of the moment

    • Optional "NEW" badge to indicate recently added content

  • Interaction:

    • Clicking on a video card opens the full video in a player view for playback.

    • Users can scroll vertically to browse additional cards.

7. Summary Flow

Main Screen → Videos Tab → Highlights Stories → Stories Viewer → Ads Banner 1 → Superstars Section → Ads Banner 2 → Best of URC Section → Video Playback
