Videos Tab Flow

The Videos tab displays video content related to the user’s favourite team, including match highlights and curated best moments.

1. Default State

  • Videos tab is selected from the My Team Tab Selector.

  • Video content is displayed in stacked sections similar to the global Videos screen.

2. Highlights Component

  • A horizontal Instagram-like stories component is shown at the top.

  • Displays highlights of the selected team’s matches.

Interaction with Highlights

  • Clicking a highlight opens the Stories Viewer, where:

    • Users can swipe through video stories.

    • Stories auto-advance similar to Instagram stories.

3. Best of [Team Name] Section

  • A horizontal card list titled “Best of Team Name” (for example, Best of Sharks).

  • Shows curated the best moments and top clips for the selected team.

Interaction

  • Clicking a card opens the Video Player / Stories Viewer with the selected clip or playlist.

4. User Flow Summary

  1. User opens My Team → Videos tab.

  2. Highlights stories are displayed at the top.

  3. User taps a highlight to view team match stories.

  4. User scrolls to Best of Team Name section.

  5. User taps a video card to watch curated team content.
